Everything you need for effortless net worth tracking

Powerful tools, local finance awareness, and complete control over your data — all in one simple, fast app.

Create Custom Asset Categories

Organize your accounts exactly how you think about your wealth — banks, mutual funds, PF, real estate, crypto, gold, or anything else. Rearrange them anytime without breaking a single formula.

Multi-Currency Support

Hold assets in INR, USD, EUR, or other major currencies — CoralMoney automatically converts them so your total net worth is always accurate.

Your Data is Yours

Export all your accounts, balances, and history to CSV anytime — no lock-in, no hidden formats. Keep a personal backup, run your own analysis, or move to another tool whenever you like.

Bring Your History In

Already tracking in a spreadsheet or another app? Import your past net worth values in minutes without recreating individual accounts — perfect for picking up where you left off.

Manual-Only Privacy

No bank connections, no scraping. You decide what to enter and when — keeping your financial details secure and under your control.

Free to Start

Get started in minutes without paying a rupee. If we add premium features later, you’ll always be able to export your data anytime.

Built for Indian Finance

Track local asset types like EPF, PPF, FDs, mutual funds, UPI balances, and gold alongside global investments in one place.

Privacy by design

  • No bank connections or scrapers
  • Sign in with email/password
  • Your data rows are scoped to your account in Firestore

Tip: never share your account; create separate logins per person.
