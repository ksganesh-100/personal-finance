Privacy Policy

Effective date: August 2025

CoralMoney (“we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how it’s used.

Data we collect

  • Account information (email, name if provided) when you sign up.
  • Manually entered financial data (account names, balances, groups, etc.).

How we use data

Your data is stored securely in our Firebase database and is only accessible to you when signed in. We use it solely to provide the features of the CoralMoney app.

What we don’t do

  • No bank connections or screen-scraping.
  • No selling or sharing your data with third parties.
  • No use of your financial data for advertising.

Data deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data from within the app at any time.

Contact

If you have questions, email us at support@coralmoney.in.
